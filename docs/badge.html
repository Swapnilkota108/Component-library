Badge

Badges are helpful to display numbers or status for an avatar. Badges are small circles, positioned at the corner of an element.

Badge on avatars

Badges on avatar can be online, offline, dnd created by badge-online, badge-offline, badge-dnd respectively.

Badge on text

Badges can be applied on text using badge-text class.
